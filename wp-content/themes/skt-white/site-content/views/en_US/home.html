• Introduction

    As a globalized company with modern and advanced management concept, Britway was incorporated in 2000 in the province of British Columbia, Canada. By taking advantages of experienced management team who used to work in well know multinational companies for many years , Professional staff as well as wide connections with global industries, we have provided most cost effective solutions to our global customers in various sectors such as Electronics and telecommunications, Metals and metallurgical , Energy Conservation and Environment protection …etc.

    Over the past 15 years, Britway has gradually become a bridge connecting the east and the west and the tie among product, talent people and working capital. With the business grows and expansion, especially the grows of resources business, A specialized entity - Britway Resources Inc was incorporated in 2015 in order to be more focus on resources business and provide more professional services to our customers.

    Britway Resources Inc is involved in the broad meaning resources integration business. In addition regular resources trading, We are also involved in the integration services in Technologies, Human resources and Capital as well. We are able to provide with total comprehensive solutions to our customers with our rich experience and competitive advantages, including Investment consultancies, direct & indirect investment in entire phases of a company growing from Angel investment, Venture capital to Private Equity investments.

    We take natural resources, network resources and financial resources as a broad meaning resources. Through our code of conduct: People-oriented, Resources integration, Create value and Satisfy customers, we commit to provide our customers with best total solutions .

    Britway Resources Inc is expecting to be your trustworthy partner in your Business growing way to the successful goal !
