Titanium with element symbol Ti and atomic number is 22 is a silvery-white transition metal , Its main features are light in weight and high in strength, good corrosion resistance to sea water, aqua regia and chlorine..etc. Therefore it is know as “space metal “ for its thermo stability, low and high temperature resistance, acidproof, Alkali-resisting as well as other stable chemical properties.

Ferro Titanium is alloy of Titanium and iron , It is used in steelmaking as a cleansing agent for iron and steel.
