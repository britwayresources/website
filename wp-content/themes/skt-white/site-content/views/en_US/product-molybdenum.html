Molybdenum with element symbol Mo and atomic number 42 is a silvery white metal with hard and tough feature.

Ferro Molybdenum is the main product of molybdenum alloy with 50%-60% Molybdenum. One of the primary benefits adding Ferro Molybdenum to an alloy is that it harden properties ,which makes steel extremely strong and at the same time weldable, Additionally, adding Ferro Molybdenum to an alloy can increase corrosion resistance. Therefore it is main agent to produce HSLA (High Strength-Low Alloy) steel and stainless Steel.
