Tungsten (Wolfram ) with element symbol W, Atomic number 74 is an important alloying element in high-speed and other tool steels, and is used to a lesser extent in some stainless and structural steels.

Ferro Tungsten is an alloying agent in steel making and it is also a remarkably robust alloy because of its high melting point. Wolfram has the second highest melting point after carbon.
