    Vanadium Alloy

    Vanadium with element symbol V and atomic number 23 is a silver-grey metal with good tractility and hardness, corrosion preventive, resistance to water, hydrochloric acid and sulfuric acid.     Titanium Alloy

    Titanium with element symbol Ti and atomic number is 22 is a silvery-white transition metal , Its main features are light in weight and high in strength, good corrosion resistance to sea water, aqua regia and chlorine..etc. Therefore it is know as “space metal “ for its thermo stability, low and high temperature resistance, acidproof, Alkali-resisting as well as other stable chemical properties.

    More

    Tungsten Alloy

    Tungsten (Wolfram ) with element symbol W, Atomic number 74 is an important alloying element in high-speed and other tool steels, and is used to a lesser extent in some stainless and structural steels. Ferro Tungsten is an alloying agent in steel making and it is also a remarkably robust alloy because of its high melting point. Wolfram has the second highest melting point after carbon.

    More

    Molybdenum Alloy

    Molybdenum with element symbol Mo and atomic number 42 is a silvery white metal with hard and tough feature. Ferro Molybdenum is the main product of molybdenum alloy with 50%-60% Molybdenum. One of the primary benefits adding Ferro Molybdenum to an alloy is that it harden properties ,which makes steel extremely strong and at the same time weldable, Additionally, adding Ferro Molybdenum to an alloy can increase corrosion resistance. Therefore it is main agent to produce HSLA (High Strength-Low Alloy) steel and stainless Steel.
